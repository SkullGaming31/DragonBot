# Privacy Policy for DragonBot

Last updated: 2026-07-06

This Privacy Policy explains what data DragonBot (the “Bot”) collects, how it is used, and your choices.

## 1. Data We Collect

- **Discord account identifiers:** When the Bot operates in a server it may store identifiers such as your Discord user ID, username, discriminator, and server (guild) ID to provide features and remember settings.
- **Messages and content:** The Bot may read messages or content you post when necessary for commands, moderation, logging, or configured features. Messages are not collected unless needed by a feature.
- **Configuration and settings:** Server- or user-specific settings (prefixes, roles, opt-ins) may be stored.
- **Logs and analytics:** The Bot may collect operational logs and anonymous analytics (errors, usage counts) to improve reliability.
- **Third-party data:** Data shared via third-party integrations (e.g., Twitch, YouTube, webhook payloads) will be processed as necessary for the integration.

## 2. How We Use Data

- To provide, maintain, and improve Bot features.
- To perform moderation actions when configured by server administrators.
- To respond to support requests and investigate abuse.
- To run analytics and debugging for reliability and performance.

## 3. Data Retention

- Data is retained only as long as needed to provide Bot features or to comply with legal obligations. Retention periods may vary for different data types (e.g., short-term logs vs persistent server configuration).

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

- The Bot may share or transmit data to third-party services required to provide integrations (APIs, analytics providers). Such sharing is governed by the third party's privacy terms.
- The Bot will not sell personal data.

## 5. Security

- Reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards are used to protect data. However, no system is fully secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Choices

- Server administrators control whether the Bot is present and how it is configured.
- If a feature stores or displays user data, server admins may be able to delete or clear that data via the Bot's commands or configuration UI (if available).
- To request deletion of data associated with your Discord ID, contact the maintainers (see Contact below). Provide your Discord ID and the servers involved.

## 7. Children

- The Bot is not intended for users under the age required by Discord terms. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 8. International Transfers

- Data may be processed in jurisdictions other than your location. By using the Bot, you consent to transfer, storage, and processing in these jurisdictions.

## 9. Legal Requests

- The Bot may disclose information in response to lawful requests by public authorities or to protect rights, property, or safety.

## 10. Changes to this Policy

- We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be indicated by updating the "Last updated" date.

## 11. Contact

- To ask questions, request data deletion, or request copies of data associated with your Discord ID, contact the maintainers via the project repository or the support contact in the README.
